We utilize asymmetric-key encryption, a highly secure encryption method, to keep your passwords safe. This encryption technique uses a pair of keys â€“ a public key and a private key. The public key is used to encrypt the passwords, while the private key, which only you have access to, is used to decrypt them. This means that even if the encrypted passwords are intercepted, they remain unintelligible without the private key, providing an added layer of protection.
